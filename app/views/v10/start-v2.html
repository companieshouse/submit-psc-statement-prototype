Provide identity verification details for a PSC, or request an extension

Use this service to provide a Companies House personal code for a person with significant control (PSC). This is to confirm that the PSC on our records has verified their identity.

You can also use this service to:

  • check when a company's PSCs need to provide their personal codes
  • request an extension, if a PSC needs more time

You cannot use this service if a PSC has their personal information protected at Companies House (or is waiting for the results of an application).

When you need to provide verification details

This is different for everyone, and depends on whether the PSC was registered with Companies House before or after identity verification requirements were introduced on [date].

If they were registered as a PSC before [date], they have 14 days starting from the company’s confirmation statement date to provide their personal code. They cannot do it before then.

If they were registered as a PSC after [date], they have 14 days starting from the date they were registered.

To see the deadline dates for a company's PSCs, select the ‘Start’ button and sign in to this service.
! Warning If a PSC does not comply with identity verification requirements, they will be committing an offence and may have to pay a financial penalty or fine. We may also display a note against their name on the public register.

Requesting an extension

If a PSC cannot provide their verification details by their deadline, you may be able to request an extension of 14 days. You need to do this before the PSC's deadline has passed.

To request an extension, select the ‘Start’ button and sign in to this service.

Before you start

To use this service, you'll need:

  • to sign in to a GOV.UK One Login account – if you do not have one, you can create one on the next screen
  • the company number

If you’re providing verification details for a PSC, you’ll need their Companies House personal code.

It can take up to 10 minutes to provide verification details or request an extension.

What is a Companies House personal code?

This is an 11 character code that is given to a person after they have verified their identity for Companies House.

Find out more about verifying your identity for Companies House.
