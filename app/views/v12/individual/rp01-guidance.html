Correcting the PSC date of birth on the Companies House register

To correct the date of birth on the Companies House register, someone authorised to file for this company will need to submit a paper corrections form. They should follow this process.

  1. Print and fill in the RP01 form: ‘Replace a document not meeting requirements’.
  2. Print and fill in the PSC01 form with the correct date of birth: ‘Give notice of individual person with significant control’.
  3. Send the completed paper forms together by post to Companies House.

Once received, the submitted updates may take up to a couple of weeks to show on the register. When the PSC details are up to date, you will be able to successfully provide identity verification details for the PSC.
